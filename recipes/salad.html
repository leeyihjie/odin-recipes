Apple Spinach Salad Recipe

Description of Apple Spinach Salad Recipe

The addition of nuts and fruit makes this salad extra special.

Additional Time: 10 mins, Total Time: 10 mins, Servings: 2, Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients required:

  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 medium apple, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  • 2 tablespoons toasted PLANTERS Pecans, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons KRAFT LIGHT DONE RIGHT! House Italian Reduced Fat Dressing

Steps to take:

  1. Toss ingredients with dressing in large bowl.
  2. (Optional) Spread nuts in single layer in shallow baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees F 8 to 10 min. or until lightly toasted, stirring occasionally.
